Q: What is a blockchain?
A: A blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across many computers.

Q: What is Bitcoin?
A: Bitcoin is the first and most well-known cryptocurrency, created by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009.

Q: How does mining work in crypto?
A: Mining is the process of validating transactions and adding them to the blockchain in exchange for rewards.

Q: What is a smart contract?
A: A smart contract is a self-executing contract with the terms directly written into code, deployed on a blockchain.

Q: What is the difference between a coin and a token?
A: A coin operates on its own blockchain, while a token is built on top of another blockchain.
